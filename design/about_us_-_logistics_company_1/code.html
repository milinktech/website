About FOCUS: Connecting Your World

Delivering excellence in global logistics with reliability and innovation at our core. We are your trusted partner in a complex, interconnected world.

Our Mission

To provide seamless, efficient, and innovative logistics solutions that empower businesses to thrive in a global marketplace, ensuring every shipment is handled with precision and care.

Our Vision

To be the world's most customer-centric logistics company, pioneering sustainable practices and advanced technology to shape the future of global trade and connectivity.

Our Journey

2005

Foundation of FOCUS

FOCUS was established with a small team and a big vision: to simplify logistics for local businesses.

2012

First International Expansion

Opened our first international office in Singapore, marking our first step towards becoming a global player.

2018

Tech Integration

Launched our proprietary tracking platform, providing clients with real-time visibility and data analytics.

2023

Sustainable Logistics Initiative

Committed to a 50% reduction in carbon emissions by introducing a fleet of electric vehicles and optimizing routes.
